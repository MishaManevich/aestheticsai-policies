Privacy Policy for Aesthetics AI

Effective Date: December 2025
Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Aesthetics AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Aesthetics AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account and Authentication Information

When you sign in using Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Authentication tokens (Apple ID token or Google ID token) for secure authentication
  • Email address (if you choose to share it with Apple Sign-In)
  • Unique user identifier (UUID) assigned by our system
  • Display name (if provided)

Note: With Apple Sign-In, you can choose to hide your email address. We respect your privacy choices.

1.2 Profile Information

During onboarding and profile setup, you provide:

  • Date of birth
  • Height (in cm or feet/inches)
  • Weight (in kg or lbs)
  • Training experience level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
  • Training frequency (days per week: 4, 5, or 6)
  • Primary fitness goal
  • Training duration (how long you've been training)
  • Available equipment (gym, home, bodyweight)
  • Injuries or physical limitations (optional, free-form text)

1.3 Physique Photos

When you perform a scan, we collect:

  • Front photo of your physique
  • Back photo of your physique (for full scans)
  • Photo metadata (timestamp, device information)

Important: These photos contain sensitive personal information. They are stored securely and are only accessible to you and our AI processing systems.

1.4 AI Analysis Results

We generate and store:

  • Physique scores (overall score, muscle group ratings 0-100)
  • Aesthetic metrics (V-taper, symmetry, definition, proportions, potential)
  • Personalized feedback (text recommendations for each muscle group)
  • Body fat percentage estimates (derived from visual analysis)
  • Percentile rankings (comparison to other users)
  • Progress tracking data (comparisons over time)

1.5 Workout Plans

We generate and store:

  • Custom workout splits (generated from your physique ratings)
  • Exercise recommendations (specific exercises, sets, reps)
  • Training schedule (based on your availability)

1.6 Usage and Analytics Data

We automatically collect:

  • App usage statistics (features used, screens viewed, time spent)
  • Device information (iOS version, device model, device identifier)
  • Crash reports and error logs (to improve app stability)
  • Performance metrics (app load times, response times)
  • Purchase and subscription data (subscription status, purchase history)

1.7 Location Data

We do NOT collect precise location data. We do not track your geographic location.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Provision

We use your data to:

  • Analyze your physique using AI technology (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini Vision API)
  • Generate physique ratings and scores for each muscle group
  • Create personalized workout plans based on your physique analysis
  • Track your progress over time by comparing scans
  • Provide feedback and recommendations to improve your physique
  • Manage your account and subscription

2.2 AI Processing

Photo Analysis:

  • Your photos are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini Vision API for analysis
  • OpenAI processes your photos according to their Data Usage Policy
  • OpenAI does NOT use your photos to train their models (per our API agreement)
  • OpenAI automatically deletes photos from their servers after processing (typically within minutes)
  • We store copies of your photos in Supabase Storage for your access and future scans
  • Analysis results are stored securely in our database

Workout Plan Generation:

  • Your physique ratings are used to generate custom workout splits
  • The AI prioritizes your weakest muscle groups for targeted training
  • Plans are regenerated each time you complete a new scan

2.3 Service Improvement

By default, your scan photos may be reviewed by our team to:

  • Improve AI accuracy and scoring algorithms
  • Fix analysis errors and enhance model performance
  • Train and refine our machine learning systems
  • Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Develop new features

You can opt-out anytime:

  • Go to Settings → "Help Improve AI Accuracy" → Toggle OFF
  • Opting out only affects future scans
  • Contact us at misha@aestheticsai.app to request removal of past data used for improvement

Note: Even if you opt-out, your photos are still processed for your personal analysis. The opt-out only affects whether your photos are used for improving our AI systems.

2.4 Communication

We use your information to:

  • Send important updates about the app (service notifications)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you of new features (with your permission)
  • Send subscription-related communications (renewal reminders, payment confirmations)

Marketing Communications:

  • We do NOT send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt-in
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

2.5 Analytics and Performance

We use analytics data to:

  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Identify bugs and performance issues
  • Improve user experience
  • Make data-driven product decisions

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Location: Your data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Row-level security policies ensure only you can access your data
  • Backups: Regular automated backups are performed for data recovery

4.2 Photo Storage

  • Storage Location: Photos are stored in Supabase Storage (private buckets)
  • Access: Only accessible to authenticated users (you) and our AI processing systems
  • Retention: Photos are retained as long as your account is active
  • Deletion: You can delete photos at any time, and they will be permanently removed

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In and Google Sign-In
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access logging and monitoring
  • Secure API keys and credentials management

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4.4 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup Retention: Backups may retain deleted data for up to 90 days for recovery purposes
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain certain data longer if required by law

5.4 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Obtain confirmation of whether we process your data and access to that data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data (via Settings → Profile)
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format (contact us to request)

Important Limitations:

  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can restrict optional processing (data improvement reviews), but you cannot restrict core processing (photo analysis, account management, service delivery) as it is necessary to provide the App's core functionality. Restricting core processing would prevent you from using the App.
  • Right to Object: You can object to optional processing (data improvement reviews), but you cannot object to core processing (photo analysis, account management, service delivery) as it is necessary to provide the App's core functionality. Objecting to core processing would prevent you from using the App.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for optional processing (data improvement reviews), but withdrawing consent for core processing would require deleting your account and ceasing use of the App.

To exercise these rights, contact us at misha@aestheticsai.app with "GDPR Request" in the subject line.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR):

  • Contract Performance: Core processing (photo analysis, account management, service delivery) is necessary to provide the service you requested
  • Consent: Optional processing (data improvement reviews) is based on your consent, which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and ensure security
